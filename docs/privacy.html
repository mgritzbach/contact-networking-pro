CONTACT NETWORKING PRO

Privacy, without fine print.

Effective July 17, 2026

No account. No ads. No analytics. No contact data sent to a server.

Contact Networking Pro is designed to perform its core functions on your Android device.

Information stored on your device

The app stores the contact profile and QR content you choose to save in its private local storage. Widget and Quick Settings preferences are also stored locally. Android device backup is disabled for this app.

Camera and business-card scanning

Camera permission is requested only when you choose to scan a card. The photo is placed temporarily in the app cache and processed on-device using Google ML Kit Text Recognition. The temporary photo is deleted after processing or cancellation. The app does not upload card images or recognized text.

Sharing and contacts

When you share a QR image, Android's system share sheet sends it only to the destination you select. When you save a scanned contact, the app opens Android's contact insertion screen so you can review and confirm the data. The app does not request permission to read your address book.

Network access and third parties

The app does not request Android's internet permission and contains no advertising or analytics SDK. Google ML Kit's bundled text-recognition model performs recognition locally. Links you deliberately open, such as this policy, are handled by your chosen browser.

Lock-screen and widget behavior

The optional Quick Settings tile can display your selected QR over the lock screen after you tap it. It does not unlock the device or bypass security. Home-screen widgets may expose the QR you select to anyone who can see your home screen; choose this option only when that is appropriate for you.

Deleting your data

Use Settings → Reset all app data, clear the app's storage in Android Settings, or uninstall the app. Each option removes the locally stored profile and QR data.

Children

The app is a professional utility and is not directed to children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes and contact

Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. Questions can be submitted through the project's GitHub issue tracker.
